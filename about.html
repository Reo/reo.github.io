I have been interested in security for as far back as I remember, as I child I would get into trouble for finding ways to get inside places I shouldn't have. Besides security I have also had a fascination with the structure present in modern algebra. More precisely, I am studying algebraic geometry. The intersection of these topics have led me to find a particular interest in cryptography when it comes to cyber security. In my time at the University of Toronto I have since done a research project on security protocols and attacks on Elliptic Curve Cryptography.
