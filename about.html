I am a Teaching Assistant (TA) in the department of Computer and Mathematical Sciences at the University of Toronto Scarborough. I have assisted in the instruction of computer science and math courses; particularly courses teaching how the C programming language behaves under the hood.

My main focus is on cryptographic protocols which help form the foundation for modern communication (and more). I find it extremely rewarding to get a deeper understanding of technologies new and old or learn new skills altogether. In my spare time, I play music in an ensemble and love going to concerts.
